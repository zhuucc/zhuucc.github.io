INTRODUCTION

The global manufacturing industry is mainly developing towards Industry 4.0 with intelligent technology, combining the Internet of Things and AI artificial intelligence to realize intelligent manufacturing. The main purpose of Industry 4.0 is to upgrade precision machinery to smart machinery, by integrating various smart technologies to enable it to have various smart functions, and to provide the best solution to establish a differentiated competitive advantage.

In order to assist the manufacturing industry to introduce smart machinery, AI artificial intelligence, Internet of Things, cloud computing, big data analysis and other methods can be used to build smart production lines that require high efficiency, high quality, and high flexibility (customization).Through the integration of virtual and real systems, all technologies are connected in series and the intelligent networked manufacturing service system is introduced to achieve applications such as equipment networking, production management visualization and intelligence, and thus enhance the brand's international competitiveness.

Active Smart Function : Thermal Compensation Function, Spindle Load/Speed Monitoring, Alert Notification, Instant Processing Time Evaluation

Product Management : Utilization Statistics, MES Integrated Management, Equipment Status Record, Maintenance Reminder, Tool Table Management
