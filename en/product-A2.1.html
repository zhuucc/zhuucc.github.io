Thermal Compensation Function
Product description

According to the ASME B5.54 standard, measure the thermal displacement of the machine tool spindle and structure relative to the worktable, and build a database of thermal displacement characteristic curves based on this, and for temperature rise parameters, time parameters and thermal displacement, Establish a thermal compensation mathematical model to solve the problem of thermal isplacement caused by machine tool cutting.

Product description

After the thermal compensation function is installed on the machine, the instrument will be used for measurement to confirm the accuracy of the machine, and the improvement range can be up to 80%.
